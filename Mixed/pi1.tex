


 Ramanujan formula for $\pi$:

 Each term of this series produces an additional eight
 correct digits:
                        $$
        {1\over\pi}=
        {2\sqrt 2\over 9801}
        \sum^\infty_{k=0}
                {
          (4k)!(1103+26,390 k)
             \over
             (k!)^4 396^{4k}                        
                 }        $$
